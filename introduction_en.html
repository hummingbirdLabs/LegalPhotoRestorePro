Features

Eight Powerful Tools, One App

From faded family photos to low-resolution shots, PhotoRestore Pro covers every restoration scenario.

🗃️

Old Photo Restoration AI

Bring severely aged or faded photos back to life. A full AI pipeline — denoising, color enhancement, and 4× upscaling — is applied in sequence for the highest possible quality output.

⚡

Fast Old Photo Restoration

Instantly clean up lightly aged photos using traditional algorithms only. No AI wait time — results appear in milliseconds, perfect for photos that just need a quick touch-up.

🔍

Photo Upscale 4× AI

Enlarge any photo to four times its original resolution using a Real-ESRGAN model. Recovers sharp details from compressed or low-resolution images with remarkable clarity.

🎨

AI Colorization AI

Transform black-and-white photographs into natural, vivid color images. The AI model analyzes scene context to produce plausible, life-like color without manual input.

✨

Color Enhancement

Choose from eight distinct visual presets — from Natural & Crisp to Cinematic Film — to instantly lift the contrast, saturation, and warmth of any color photo.

🔧

Scratch & Crease Repair

Detect and remove physical scratches and fold lines from scanned photos using morphological inpainting. Restores visual continuity across damaged areas without leaving artifacts.

🎞️

9-Style Filter Grid

Preview nine artistic filters side-by-side in a scrollable thumbnail strip and pick your favorite. Filters include Black & White, Vintage Warm, Cool Teal, Film Grain, and more.

✂️

Crop & Convert

Crop photos with precision using preset aspect ratios or a free selection, then export to PNG, JPEG, BMP, or TIFF — all in one step with no external tools required.

💧

Batch Watermark

Apply text or image watermarks to up to 50 photos at once. Configure position, opacity, size, and rotation, then export the entire batch in a single operation.
